What Is A Payoneer Prepaid MasterCard?

http://help.elance.com/hc/en-us/articles/203735633-What-is-a-Payoneer-Prepaid-MasterCard-

The Elance Prepaid MasterCard is a debit card that has a prepaid limit and can be used in most places that MasterCard is accepted. Additionally, you are also able to withdraw funds from your available balance at any ATM that contains the MasterCard symbol.*  Payoneer Prepaid MasterCard allows you to have faster access to your cash, can cost less than other withdrawal types and offers protection to the cardholder against unauthorized charges.

Elance 预付卡是一种借记卡，有预付限额，几乎万事达信用卡能用的地方它都能用。另外，你也可以在任何包含万事达信用卡的 ATM 提款机上取款。Payoneer 预付卡能够让你更快的提取到现金，费用比其他提款方式更少，而且对持卡人提供保护以防止未授权的扣款。
